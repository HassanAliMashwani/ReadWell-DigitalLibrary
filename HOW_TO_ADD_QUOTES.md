### Method 1: From Browse Page (Recommended)

1. **Navigate to Browse Page**

2. **Find a Book**
   - Search for a book using the search bar

3. **Open Reading Progress Modal**
   - On the book card, click the **"Reading Progress"** button
   - The button has a book icon: ðŸ“– Reading Progress
   - A modal window will open

4. **Scroll to "Add Quote" Section**
   - In the modal, scroll down past the reading progress form
   - You'll see a section titled **"Add Quote"**

5. **Enter Your Quote**
   - **Quote Text** (Required): Type or paste your favorite quote in the text area
   - **Chapter** (Optional): Enter the chapter number where you found the quote
   - **Page** (Optional): Enter the page number where you found the quote

6. **Save the Quote**
   - Click the **"Add Quote"** button (with a âž• icon)
   - You'll see a success message: "Quote saved!"
   - The quote will appear in the "Saved Quotes" section above

7. **View Your Quotes**
   - All saved quotes for this book will appear in the "Saved Quotes" section
   - Each quote shows the text, chapter, and page number
   - You can delete quotes by clicking the delete button (ðŸ—‘ï¸) next to each quote

### Method 2: From My Library Page

1. **Go to My Library**
   - Click on **"My Library"** in the navigation menu
   - Or go to: `http://localhost:8000/library.html`

2. **Select a Book**
   - Find a book you've saved (favorite or bookmarked)
   - Click the **"Reading Progress"** button on the book card

3. **Follow Steps 4-7 from Method 1**
   - The process is the same as adding quotes from the Browse page

---

### Method 3: From Profile Page

1. **Go to Profile**
   - Click on **"Profile"** in the navigation menu
   - Or go to: `http://localhost:8000/profile.html`

2. **View Currently Reading Books**
   - In the "Currently Reading" section, you'll see all books with saved progress
   - Click **"View Quotes"** button on any book

3. **Add New Quote**
   - This will redirect you to the Browse page with the reading progress modal open
   - Follow Steps 4-7 from Method 1

## â“ Troubleshooting

### "Please login to track reading progress"
- **Solution**: Make sure you're logged in. Go to Dashboard and sign in.

### Quote not saving
- **Solution**: 
  - Make sure you entered text in the "Quote Text" field
  - Check your internet connection
  - Refresh the page and try again

### Can't see "Reading Progress" button
- **Solution**: 
  - Make sure you're logged in
  - The button appears on book cards in Browse and My Library pages

### Quotes not showing in Profile
- **Solution**: 
  - Make sure you saved quotes for at least one book
  - Refresh the Profile page
  - Check browser console for errors (F12)
